Serving Humanity Through Medical Fraternity

The glorious Journey of Grandix's began in 1996 with the vision that  “Building brands is the only way to build a everlasting future“.

Grandix was the first in the country to launch a double-drug therapy (FDC) for the management of diabetes and hypertension.

Grandix was also the first in India to introduce a mega brand- “RENERVE and RENERVE PLUS “  * offered as soft-gel capsules and injections for neuropathy, which eventually turned out to be a “Hundred crore brand".
